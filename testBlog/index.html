About us!

This project is a result of an international partnership between Refugees Welcome Porto (Portugal), Wasla (Germany); United Society of Balkans (Greece) and Inserta Andalucia ( Spain), funded by the Erasmus + Programme.

​The platform at your service its a project that aims to reunite all services available for refugees and migrants in Portugal and Germany and legal information about asylum procedures in this countries.
